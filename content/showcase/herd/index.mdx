I co-founded Herd in early 2020. Our goal is to make support groups accessible and affordable, by creating a centralized platform where people can find and book time with their group.

With a few clicks you can see all the online and in-person groups being held by our long list of licensed professionals and join their sessions seamlessly. For providers we’ve simplified the marketing and administrative side so that they can focus entirely on the care of their members.
